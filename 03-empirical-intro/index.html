The 100 Pics Strategy

Fashion Instagrammers

The three fashion personae chosen below represent the wide spectrum of fashion bloggers, from Chiara Ferragni, currently the most followed fashion blogger worldwide, to Susie Bubble, a pioneer in the industry, and Claartje Rose, a young emerging blogger from Amsterdam. The 100 Pics Strategy reflects how audience resonance is a cultivated negotiation between displays of personal intimacy and lucrative sponsorships. In particular, the 100 Pics Strategy will show how themes of motherhood, romance and friendship are curated narratives that each blogger employs in order to advance their fashionable personae.
