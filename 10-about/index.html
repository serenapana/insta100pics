About Us

Serena Panariti

Serena is a designer, visual artist and researcher from Italy. She studied Design and Multimedia at IUAV University of Venice, with an Erasmus period at IADE Creative University in Lisbon. She is always on the move to achieve something new to learn, possibly experiencing new intercultural environments. Recently besed in Amsterdam, she worked at the PublishingLab by investigating the interrelation of Fashion and Social Media. Her goal is to be out of the box, while always crafting new ways to rethink herself.

Claudia Giralt Monedero

Claudia is an artist and designer, also known as A cAt. She studied graphic design in Spain, Digital Media Design in England, with a master in Experimental Publishing, at Piet Zwart, Rotterdam, while working as an intern at PublishingLab. Apart from working as a graphic designer, the areas she explores are glitch, the subjectivity of reality, and the mere meaning of life. Glitch is approached as a mean to transmit other concepts. She tries to go out of social constructed paradigms, questioning the main structures of believes on which society is build. Her approach is self-reflexive and self-explorative, with the idea that all answers can be found in oneself.



PublishingLab

Part of the Amsterdam University of Applied Science and of the Institute for Network Cultures, the PublishingLab is an applied research lab focusing on the intersection of publishing practices and digital technologies, creating output for screen, paper, and all imaginable in-betweens.

The PublishingLab works with young enthusiasts, still studying or recently graduated, that come from different backgrounds and employ design, hacking, writing and many other means to produce experiments with digital publishing work ows, publication formats, reader interaction and user interface design. The PublishingLab also works with various partners, ranging from small publishers, government institutions, to large international companies in the eld of digital media.
