Emerging from their modest beginnings on weblogs and anonymous online forums, fashion bloggers have experienced a meteoric rise. The prestigious runaways and exclusive designer events, before restricted for the exceedingly privileged, are now flooded by electronic glow of phones and tablets. Tracing this peculiar and unlikely ascent, and understanding their success, means looking closely at the history of fashion blogging and why Instagram emerged as the ideal tool to reshuffle the industry.
