Digital Identities

Fashionable Personae

For many, what distinguishes bloggers from the authoritative editors at Vogue or Harper's Bazaar and their commanding stoop is the personal voice. While traditional magazines aspire for journalistic integrity and fair criticism, bloggers sacrifice this and indulge followers into the glamorous and fantastical allure of the fashion world. Where magazines strive for journalism and criticism, fashion Instagrammers invite the voyeurism of social media and blur any distinctions between their personal life and their blogging identity. Financed through gifts from designer labels and intimidate partnerships with particular brands, Instagrammers bring an irresistible intimacy to the fashion world that seduce followers into experiencing the converted front row of runways.

IMAGE

Coming from the early blogging traditions, Instagrammers have adopted the platform as a visual diary, inviting users into privileged glimpses of their lives, alongside the glamorous adventures that come as fashion celebrities. And while fashion commentary remains the central narrative of their content, it’s infused with carefully revealed personal elements. Pregnancy, friendship, romantic relationships and family become the intimate backdrop that permeates the affable peculiarity of their fashion commentary.

Looking at Instagram through this lens brings forward the question of identity, as well as the importance of self-presentation and strategies of identity formation. While often understood as a permanent and enduring concept, identity should be viewed more as an ephemeral and continuously performative project. This understanding therefore can enlighten us on how Instagrammers leverage the platform’s unique politics and structure to construct a digital identity that resonates with followers. Responding to followers, collaborating with other bloggers, using particular #hashtags and repeating specific selfie poses are all important interactions that affirm a digital identity. As a result, this Fashionable Personae produces a crafted intimacy; where Instagrammers leverage the platform’s specific functionality in order to seamlessly blur the boundaries between personal and public personas.

As a result, followers can develop intimate relationships with Instagrammers, a particular phenomenon social scientists coin as parasocial relationships. Originally developed for ‘media figure’ such as presenters, actors and celebrities, parasocial relationships describe the emotional relationships audiences develop with mass-media figures.
